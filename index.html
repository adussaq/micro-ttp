Blood culture time to Positive by Tube

Blood culture time to Positive by Patient Visit/Organism

For the following analysis every positive culture for every patient was grouped based on being within 7 days from another draw. The concept for this is that it would represent the course of single infection.

The "Positive Tubes" figure is the number of positive tubes on a given stay for a given patient. The "Positive Tubes With Organism" are the number of tubes with a given organism. For example if someone had 4 positive tubes, 3 with S. aureus and 1 with S. aureus/E. coli then one count would be added to the 4 positive tubes category and one count (S. aureus) would be added to the 4 positive tubes with organisms and one count (E. coli) would be added to the 1 positive tubes with organism.
